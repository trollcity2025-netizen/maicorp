Our Story

MaiCorp was founded on the principle that technology should serve humanity — not the other way around. We build digital platforms that address real-world challenges, combining deep industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to create solutions that drive meaningful impact.

Our team doesn't just write code. We think strategically about every project, understanding the business context, the user needs, and the technical constraints before we build a single line. This approach ensures that what we deliver isn't just functional — it's valuable.

We've built and launched our own platforms, which means we understand the full lifecycle from concept to scale. That experience shapes how we work with every client.
