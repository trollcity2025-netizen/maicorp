Our Story

Built From Real Platform Experience.

MaiCorp was created with a simple belief: technology should make business easier, not harder. A good website should do more than look nice. It should help customers understand what you offer, submit the right information, pay securely, and give the business owner a clear place to manage what happens next.

We are not building from theory. MaiCorp is also the foundation behind MAI-branded platform ideas and internal systems, giving us hands-on experience with authentication, dashboards, payments, media platforms, creator tools, and operational workflows.

That experience shapes how we serve clients. Whether someone needs a simple service website or a full platform, we think through the customer journey, admin workflow, payment path, data structure, and long-term growth from the start.

How We Work

A Clear Build Process From Package to Launch.

The goal is to make the buying process feel simple for the client and organized for MaiCorp.

1. Choose a Package

Clients select the website or platform package that matches their business type, budget, and required functionality.

2. Submit Project Details

Customers provide business info, service details, preferred features, design notes, and additional instructions before the build starts.

3. Pay Through PayPal

Checkout is built around PayPal so orders are tracked through a clear payment flow before work begins.

4. Build the Website

MaiCorp creates the public website, key pages, forms, and admin-side workflow based on the selected package.

5. Review and Adjust

The client receives one free UI change after delivery, helping finalize the visual direction without endless revision confusion.

6. Launch and Manage

The site goes live, and the client can use their admin tools to manage orders, bookings, messages, and customer activity.
